ABOUT US

Dordorian Estate Company is a Nigerian based real estate firm located in Yenagoa, Bayelsa State with a mission to meet and exceed the investment needs of our clients through the sale of lands and homes in suitable locations, property development, and facility management. With a robust team of real estate professionals, lawyers and business developers, we ensure that our clients' real estate needs are met and exceeded. Dordorian Estate Company is a subsidiary of Dordorian Concept Limited a human capacity development and consulting company.

3.Property Development and Renovation- with our team of developers, we give you beautiful architectural masterpiece befitting your status and budget. We also renovate homes, office complexes etc for clients upon agreed payment terms.
